About

An Unmanned Aircraft System Project

Aim

The goal was to build a practical UAS that helps in measure the gas emission data over a wet marshy land in Manchester, UK.

Obstacles

  • * The gust
  • * the rainy weather of Manchester
  • * Clearing the pedestrian around the place
  • * Using the resources correctly with minimal loss
  • * Using foam board as the structure material

Journey

The project started with an intense schedule due academic time limits.

Five members were assigned in each team. Each worked in different parts of the team. The

MATLAB and the FEA analysis part was performed by me.

The project took a lot of efforts to be honest, the first launch inside the test centre was a failure but we got up to the pace in the next launch.

During second stage, the launch in the marshy land was tough deal to face and cope up. The team faced a lot of failures such as nosecone seperation while launch, loss of autopilot in the next launch, and etc,..

After sevral losses the team decided to change the design and we made two designs this time, one failed and the other got us to the finishing line.
